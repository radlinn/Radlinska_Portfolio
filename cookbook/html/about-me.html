About Me

My inspiration for this project comes from the creativity of Aniagotuje.pl, where unique and artistic dishes bring excitement to cooking. Combining this passion with the rise of AI-generated images, I aim to create a modern platform that blends culinary creativity with technology.


The name "Overcooked" is inspired by one of my favorite games, where players must work together to run a restaurant and serve dishes—often in their most simplified form, like a hamburger made of just a bun and meat. Translating this concept into real food, my site explores how playful, experimental, and sometimes bizarre dishes can spark creativity and fit into the modern culinary world.


My goal is to inspire people to experiment with food, embrace the unexpected, and explore the world of "weird cuisine." Whether it’s playful, futuristic, or simply unusual, this site is all about making food an adventure!
